# CloudCDN Pricing (2026)

## Free Tier (Open Source)
- **Target:** Personal projects, open-source software, and hobbyists.
- **Cost:** $0/month — free forever.
- **Bandwidth:** 10 GB/month.
- **Assets:** Unlimited static files (PNG, WebP, AVIF, SVG, ICO).
- **Formats:** Automatic WebP and AVIF conversion on upload.
- **Support:** Community forum and GitHub Issues.
- **Custom Domains:** Not included.
- **SLA:** Best-effort availability.

## Pro Tier
- **Target:** Commercial projects, startups, and high-traffic websites.
- **Cost:** $29/month.
- **Bandwidth:** 100 GB/month included. $0.05 per additional GB.
- **Assets:** Unlimited static files with priority processing.
- **Formats:** Automatic WebP and AVIF conversion with quality controls.
- **Image Optimization API:** Resize, crop, and transform images on-the-fly via URL parameters.
- **Custom Domains:** Up to 5 custom domains with automatic SSL.
- **Support:** Priority email support (24-hour response time).
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee.
- **Analytics:** Basic usage dashboard with bandwidth and request metrics.

## Enterprise Tier
- **Target:** Global platforms, media companies, and e-commerce at scale.
- **Cost:** Custom pricing — contact sales@cloudcdn.pro.
- **Bandwidth:** Unlimited.
- **Assets:** Unlimited with dedicated storage.
- **Image Optimization API:** Full suite with batch processing.
- **Custom Domains:** Unlimited custom domains.
- **Support:** Dedicated account manager, Slack channel, 1-hour response SLA.
- **SLA:** 99.99% uptime guarantee with financial credits.
- **Security:** SSO/SAML integration, audit logs, IP allowlisting.
- **Analytics:** Real-time dashboard with per-asset and per-region breakdown.

## Billing
- All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing available at 20% discount.
- Free tier requires no credit card.
- Pro tier includes a 14-day free trial.
- Overage charges are billed at the end of each billing cycle.
- Cancel anytime — no long-term contracts.

## Fair Use Policy
CloudCDN is designed for serving static assets (images, icons, fonts, documents). It is not intended for video streaming, file hosting, or application distribution. Accounts exceeding fair use may be contacted to discuss an appropriate plan.
